Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter
Rice

Descritpion

Rice is a grain that is consumed around the world and makes a great compliment to meals.

Directions

  1. Rinse your rice.
  2. Measure the rice and water.
  3. Bring the water to a boil.
  4. Add the rice.
  5. Cover and cook.
  6. Rice is done when tender aprox. 30min.
  7. Turn off the heat and let sit for a few minutes.
  8. Remove lid and fluff.
  9. Enjoy the best cooked rice ever!
